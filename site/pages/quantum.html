Introduction

Quantum computers aren't new. They have been in development since the 1980. With the long going effort they aren't near to be perfect.

How they work

The traditional computers are based on the charge of electrical current. Electrical current is a 1, no electrical current its a 0. They go trough logical gates to make the calculations. A quantum computer is based on the concept of quantum supersition. Two quantum states can be added together to make another state. They call it qubits. Quantum computers have been said to be faster than traditional ones. They can perform operations so quick they could break encrypted RSA communications.

Google quantum computer

Google has claimed to have stablished quantum supermacy. They have done a calculation infeasible on a traditional computer.
